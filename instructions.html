Solitaire(Klondike)

Stack cards from ace to king in their appropriate suit pile to win Click on the stockpile to add a card to the waste pile. Once stockpile is empty, click on the stockpile again to put to put all the cards in waste pile back into the the stockpile. Cards in the Tableau must be alternating in colors and are piled in decreasing order (king to ace)

Memory Match

Select two cards and see if they match. Be sure to remember which cards are which! If they match, they will stay revealed. If they don't match, they'll flip back around.

BlackJack

Get your card value total to be as close to, or at, 21 as you possibily can. Don't go over or you will lose! Press hit to get another card. Press stand to commit to your card value total.
